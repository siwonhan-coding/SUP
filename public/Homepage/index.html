SDG 2

Zero Hunger aims to end hunger, achieve food security, improve nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture.

What is household
food insecurity
?

Household food insecurity refers to the lack of consistentaccess to sufficient and nutritious food, which impacts the health and well-being of families.

Did you
know?

Nearly one-third ofhouseholds in Simcoe Muskoka struggle to afford enough food, up from 19% last year, highlighting a serious rise in local food insecurity.

If you or someone you know is struggling with food insecurity, visit our resources page for a list of food banks and support services in Simcoe Muskoka.
